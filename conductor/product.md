# Product Definition: CarbonIQ

## Initial Concept
CarbonIQ is an enterprise-grade **Carbon Intelligence Dashboard** for road freight logistics. It provides real-time emissions monitoring, shipment-level estimation, lane analytics, and what-if scenario modeling — all powered by a persistent AI Assistant.

## Vision
To provide freight logistics managers with actionable, real-time carbon intelligence to monitor, optimize, and reduce their fleet's environmental footprint using the GLEC Framework.

## Target Audience
- **Logistics Managers:** Seeking to monitor fleet-wide emissions and carrier performance.
- **Sustainability Officers:** Responsible for reporting and tracking carbon reduction goals.
- **Operations Leads:** Looking to optimize lane efficiency and model emission reduction scenarios.

## Key Features
- **Fleet Emissions Overview:** Real-time KPI dashboard (CO2e, Shipments, Avg/Shipment, Diesel Share) with AI-generated insights.
- **Shipment Estimator:** GLEC-based emission calculator considering distance, weight, fuel, vehicle type, and vehicle age.
- **Lane Analytics:** Risk classification for transport lanes (High/Medium/Low) based on emission intensity.
- **What-If Simulator:** Scenario modeling for fuel switches (EV/CNG), load consolidation, and route optimization.
- **CarbonIQ AI Assistant:** Context-aware chatbot for natural language queries about fleet emissions and sustainability.

## Differentiation
- **GLEC Framework Compliance:** High-accuracy emission modeling.
- **Vehicle Age Degradation:** Incorporates a unique multiplier (1.5%/year after 5 years) for more realistic estimations.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** Seamlessly integrates large language models (Qwen 72B) for data interpretation.
